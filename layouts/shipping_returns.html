Shipping Options

We use the United States Postal Service as our shipping provider because it allows us to offer competitive pricing and excellent service. Once you finalize your purchase you will receive a prompt email confirmation of your order and your tracking number (by email also) once your product goes out. Please keep in mind that it may take 24-72 hours for us to process and ship your order (will vary depending on seasonal busyness). Once a package has left our facility it is solely handled by USPS and all inquiries relating to its tracking or delivery should be directed to them (www.usps.com). If your package has not arrived within the time frame indicated in your tracking email please inquire with your local post office for a resolution before contacting our customer service department.

Return Policy

It’s very important to us that you are happy with your Third Eye Pinecone experience. We are very blessed to be able to create and sell products that we love and share that passion with our customers. Because of that comm we have a solid no-questions-asked 14 day return policy (minus shipping) on all of our products. Simply email our staff notifying us of your choice and send your return package to the address listed below. Please include a note in your return package with all of your pertinent information (name, address, email, phone number) and we will process your refund as soon as we have received and inspected your product.
